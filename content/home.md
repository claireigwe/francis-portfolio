## Capabilities

**Section label**

What I do

**Heading**

Sometimes the page is blank.  
Sometimes the problem is already on it.

**Introduction**

Either way, I know where to start.

### Write it

From a rough idea, a brief, or a product that needs the right words.

I write copy for people who have something to say, sell, explain, or make interesting.

### Shape it

Good ideas do not always arrive in good sentences.

I turn complicated, dull, or unclear material into writing people can understand and want to keep reading.

### Sharpen it

Sometimes the words are already there. They just need a better eye.

I edit for clarity, structure, flow, tone, and the small things that can quietly weaken a good piece of writing.

## Content Notes

- The hero should not include years of experience or a service list.
- The headline “I find the words. Then I make them work.” is the current lead direction, but it may still be tested against the visual direction before final lock.
- Show four featured projects on the homepage. The full Work page contains all six.
- The selected project preview excerpts may receive light punctuation editing for presentation, but their meaning and voice must remain faithful to Francis’s original work.
- The Short & Sharp lines should be treated as writing specimens, not ordinary cards.
- The Experience section should remain concise. The About page will tell the fuller professional story.
- Do not add testimonials unless genuine testimonials become available.
- Do not add client logos unless their use is appropriate and supported.
- Do not add invented metrics, briefs, process, or results.
- The exact layout, motion, imagery, and editorial treatment are not defined in this file.
