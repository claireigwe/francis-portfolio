## Content Notes

- Keep the page simple.
- The primary contact method is email.
- Add Francis’s real LinkedIn URL only after it has been confirmed.
- A contact form is useful but not essential to the first build. If implementing it adds unnecessary complexity, use email and LinkedIn only.
- If a contact form is built, it must actually work. Do not ship a decorative form.
- Do not add a booking calendar.
- Do not add an extensive project questionnaire.
- Do not ask for budget, company size, project type, or deadline before someone can make first contact.
- Do not add newsletter signup or other lead-capture mechanics.
- Do not promise a specific response time unless Francis has confirmed one he can consistently meet.
- Keep the tone warm, direct, and low-pressure.
- The exact visual composition will be defined during the visual direction and storyboard phases.
